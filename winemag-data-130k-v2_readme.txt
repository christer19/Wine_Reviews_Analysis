Dataset: Wine Reviews

Description: 
- 130k wine reviews with variety, location, winery, price, and description.
- 13 columns & 129,971 records

Fields include:
1. country -  The country that the wine is from 
2. description - Description of the wine 
3. designation -  The vineyard within the winery where the grapes that made the wine are from
4. points -  The number of points WineEnthusiast (www.wineenthusiast.com) rated the wine on a scale of 1-100
5. price -  The cost for a bottle of the wine (in USD)
6. province - The province or state that the wine is from 
7. region_1 - The wine growing area in a province or state (e.g. Napa Valley) 
8. region_2 - Sometimes there are more specific regions specified within a wine growing area (e.g. Napa inside the Napa Valley)
9. taster_name - Name of taster
10. taster_twitter_handle - Twitter user name of taster
11. title -  The title of the wine review (which often contains the vintage)
12. variety -  The type of grapes used to make the wine (e.g. Pinot Noir) 
13. winery - The winery that made the wine
